Ingredients:

* 15 oz can black beans
* 15 oz can corn
* 1 cup pico de gallo
* 1/2 tsp cumin
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1 tsp olive oil
* juice form half a lime
* 1 large handful cilantro, chopped

Instructions

1. Drain and rinse black beans. Drain corn
2. Add all ingredients to bowl and mix.
